Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 1 packet baking powder
  • 100g butter
  • 100g dark chocolate

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Melt the butter and chocolate together, then let it cool slightly.
  4. Beat the eggs and milk together, then add to the dry ingredients.
  5. Slowly fold in the melted butter and chocolate mixture.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased cake tin and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool before serving.
